    Hi and welcome to my lasercutting assignment!

    For my lasercutting assignment, I was tasked to make an accessories box to hold acessories. First I made a 3D model of the box in fusion 360






    Once the model was made, I converted every part into a dxf file. There are some letters on the side of the model because I was experimenting with different fonts to engrave my name on the box.




    Here are images of all the dxf files I used, some parts such as the front and back plates of the drawer had the exact same sketch so I used one dxf file and just placed it twice in correl draw







    Once I finished making the dxf files, I headed down to the FabLab and cut my box

    The wood and cuts in the image appear smaller than they are for some weird reason




    Once all the parts were cut, I assembled the parts together to form the box

    Here is my box in all its glory!
